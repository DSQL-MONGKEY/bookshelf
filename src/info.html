FACT

According to UNESCO data, Indonesian people really admire reading interest, only 0.001%. That is, out of 1,000 Indonesians, only 1 person is avid reader!

Indonesia is stated to be ranked 60th out of 61 countries regarding interest in reading, just below Thailand (59) and above Botswana (61). In fact, in terms of infrastructure assessment to support reading, Indonesia's ranking is above European countries.

Ironically, although interest in reading books is low, wearesocial data as of January 2017 reveals that Indonesians can stare at gadget screens for approximately 9 hours a day. Not surprisingly, in terms of fussiness on social media , Indonesians are ranked 5th in the world.

So, what is the importance of reading?

Books are a source of various information that can open our horizons about various things such as science, economics, social, culture, politics, and other aspects of life.
